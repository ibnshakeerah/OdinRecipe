Description

Just thinking about the phrases "delicious," "lip-smacking," and "crunchy" will make you think of the incredible American delicacy known as the "burger." Burgers can be made in a variety of ways, but no matter how you cook them, you'll always want more after eating them.

Prepare this easy yet delicious recipe for Vegetable Burgers for your family to enjoy this time when they want to have something tasty and satisfying at home. The health factor of this dish is increased by cooking it at home in addition to the fact that it is delicious. The nicest part of making this quick vegetable burger at home is that you can give it your own unique spin on flavors and ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices cheese slices
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 2 teaspoon powdered red chilli
  • 2 pinch powdered salt
  • 3 tablespoon breadcrumbs
  • 1 pound ground chuck beef
  • 3 tablespoon butter
  • 2 teaspoon refined oil
  • 2 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 4 leaves lettuce loose-leaf

Steps

  1. Pressure cook the carrot, peas, and sweet corn for one whistle, or until tender, to make the burger patties.
  2. To a large bowl, add the cooked vegetables, finely chopped onions, red chili powder, lemon juice, garam masala powder, salt, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. In the bowl, add the mashed potatoes and lemon juice. Make small to medium-sized patties out of the mixture.
  4. Oil in a pan is now heating up over a medium flame. Then shallow fried the patties in the oil until both sides are golden brown.
  5. Remove and set aside.
  6. Take a hamburger bun and cut it in half. The lettuce should be positioned on top of some butter.
  7. Then add the prepared vegetable patties. Add cheese, onion, tomato, and cucumber slices on top. Replace the cheese with eggless mayonnaise to make it even more delicious and healthful.
  8. Wrap it with the remaining burger bread half. Ketchup can be added on top if preferred. If necessary, affix it with a toothpick.
